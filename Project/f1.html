F1 2019 Anniverssary Edition

Is an upcoming racing video game based on the 2019 Formula One World Championship. The game is developed and published by Codemasters and is the twelfth title in the Formula One series developed by the studio. The game was announced by Codemasters on 28 March 2019. The video game will be the eleventh installment of the franchise, and it will feature all twenty-one circuits, twenty drivers and ten teams present in the 2019 Formula One World Championship. Codemasters has stated that the game was in development for nearly two years, and described it as "the most ambitious release in franchise history".
